Prioritize testing efforts based on risk, impact, and likelihood to maximize testing effectiveness within time constraints.

## ğŸ“Š Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Scoring Formula:

Risk Score = (Impact Score x Likelihood Score) / 100    

### Impact Scoring:

1. **Critical** - 10 (Severe safety risk, data loss, etc.)
2. **High** - 7 (Major functionality loss, data corruption)
3. **Medium** - 5 (Minor functionality issues, performance degradation)
4. **Low** - 3 (Minor cosmetic issues, minor performance impact)

### Likelihood Scoring:

1. **Very High** - 8 (High probability of occurrence)
2. **High** - 6 (Moderate probability)
3. **Medium** - 4 (Occasional occurrences)
4. **Low** - 2 (Rare occurrences)

### Priority Assignment:

1. **P0** - Critical, High Impact, Very High Likelihood
2. **P1** - High Impact, High Likelihood
3. **P2** - Medium Impact, Medium Likelihood
4. **P3** - Low Impact, Low Likelihood
